Mission & Impact

Led and organized a team to launch a social media platform with live broadcast capabilities for promoting student engagement in community and online entertainment during COVID-19 lockdown.

Focus: Addressing social isolation and entertainment needs of students when physical gatherings were not possible.

  • Handled contract negotiation with content creators and anchors

Community Impact

  • Promoted student engagement during isolation period
  • Provided online entertainment and social connection platform
